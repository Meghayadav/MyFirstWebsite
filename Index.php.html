Project Description

Our Project, Focuses on Two Major Problems :


1 : Delay in train schedule due to FOG
2 : Railway accident

Functions provided in our project:-


1. Sensors can be installed on the train which can identify up to a certain safe distance that whether the track is clear or not. So that in even in dense fog speed is not compromised much.

2. With the help of some sensors the animated view of train on run time displayed into the compartment of driver. That display shows the view of sufficient distance which help to the driver to maintain the appropriate speed. If a person or animal comes in the path of the train then it alerts the driver and avoids the accident.

3. A train running at 110 kmph (average speed of an express train in India) will come to a complete halt in about 2-3 minutes. During this time, the train covers a considerable distance, accurate knowledge about the location of the obstruction can be used to determine appropriate breaking force so that train can be stopped in timely manner without causing derailment due to sudden braking.

4. A high speed cameras can be installed near the wheels of a train. Accurate images of track can be captured and analyzed for cracks and defect.
